BrainSurfaceTK

Here we present BrainSurfaceTK, a tool designed for the application of deep learning techniques for brain surfaces. We implement the visualisation and manipulation of geometrical representations of brain-surfaces (using PyVista). Alongside visualization, we integrate several deep learning networks for two tasks: the regression of age, and segmentation of the brain into component regions. The following functionality is available:

  1. Visualisation of volumetric data as 2D MRI images.
  2. Visualisation of 3D segmented brain surfaces. An interactive display of the surface is provided.
  3. Integration of the deep learning networks with capabilities to predict age and segment the brain into 37 different regions.

This tool can be used by researchers, engineers, and medical professionals to inspect brain surfaces and has potential to aid diagnosis with the age-prediction and segmentation capabilities.
